Transactions

Transactions are the main functionality of the site. Each transaction represents a "line" in your credit card bill, or your bank account history page.

Removing

There is no way to remove transactions at the moment. Let me know if this is a feature you'd like to have.

Editing

You cannot edit a transaction. We consider them immutable. Instead, you can add some metadata to it, like a category, receipt or note.
